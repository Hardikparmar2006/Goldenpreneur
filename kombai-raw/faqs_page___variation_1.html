General

What is Golden preneur?
Golden preneur is India's most comprehensive green entrepreneurship platform. It is a structured, high-impact annual event now in its 5th Year (2026). The movement celebrates and supports entrepreneurs redefining sustainability across 150+ sectors.
Who is the organiser?
Golden preneur is organised by **1 Million Entrepreneurs International Forum (1MEIF)**, a Section 8 NGO, and powered by **VyapaarJagat.com**. It's founded by Dr. Pravin Parmar.
What is 1MEIF?
1 Million Entrepreneurs International Forum is a Section 8 Company (NGO) with 80G & 12A certification. They are CSR & Darpan certified, focusing on empowering entrepreneurs across India.
What is CEED?
CEED (Culinary Education & Enterprise Development Association) is our Association Partner, founded by Shri Anil Mulchandani and IFEA Founders. They bring expertise in sustainable growth and industry collaboration.
What is VyapaarJagat and why is my story published there?
VyapaarJagat.com is one of India's leading platforms for entrepreneur stories. Publishing your story here provides you with national visibility, 3rd party credibility, and a digital footprint that helps in SEO and business networking.

Awards & Nominations

Crucial Honorary vs Rated Awards?

Honorary Awards

100% Jury evaluated. **FREE** to nominate. For exceptional lifetime or sector contributions.

Rated Challenge

75% Jury + 25% Public Voting. Nominal fee (₹2k-₹5k) for premium marketing & voting links.

Is nomination really free?
**YES!** Nomination is always FREE for the Honorary track. We believe every green effort deserves a chance to be seen. Nominal charges only apply if you choose the Rated Challenge track for premium benefits and public voting features.
What are the award categories?
We have 35+ main categories including Eco-Friendly Product, Sustainable Construction, Renewable Energy, Waste Management, and MSME Excellence Awards. A total of 148+ sub-sectors are covered.
Who can apply?
Any entrepreneur, startup, MSME, NGO, or professional working in sustainability-focused sectors (150+ categories) can apply. This includes renewable energy, waste management, organic farming, green building, and more.
What does a winner receive?
Winners receive a prestigious Trophy, Certificate of Excellence, stage felicitation at a 5-star venue, national media coverage, inclusion in the Coffee Table Book, and a full pictorial story on VyapaarJagat.com.
How does public voting work?
For the Rated Challenge, once nominated, you receive a personalised voting link and social media creatives. Your network can vote for you online. This constitutes 25% of your final score, with the remaining 75% coming from our expert Jury.

Event Details

When is the event?
The Golden preneur 2026 Mega Event will take place on **Thursday, 25th June 2026**, from 2:00 PM to 9:30 PM.
Where is the event?
The venue is the prestigious **Renaissance by Marriott Ahmedabad Hotel**, Gujarat, India.
How do I register as a delegate?
You can register through our "Get Pass" button on the website. Delegate passes include access to panel discussions, networking sessions, award ceremony, and gala dinner.
Can I exhibit at the event?
Yes! We have 25+ exhibition stalls available. We offer two sizes: Standard (6x6 ft) and Premium (10x10 ft). Please contact Vishal Parmar (+91 70411 51714) for pricing and booking.
Can I speak or lead a masterclass?
We are always looking for expert speakers in the green economy. You can apply through our "Speakers" page. Selection is based on expertise, impact, and relevance to our 2026 theme: "Sustainability in Viksit Bharat".

Sponsorship

How can I become a sponsor?
We offer several tiers from ₹10,000 (Category Sponsor) to ₹2,00,000 (Title Sponsor). Benefits include naming rights, stage branding, attendee data, speaking slots, and premium features in the Coffee Table Book. Contact Vishal Parmar to discuss the right fit for your brand.

Coffee Table Book

How can I get featured in the Coffee Table Book?
You can apply for an "Inside Feature Story" starting at ₹5,000 (Members) to ₹15,000 (Open). Ad slots and cover stories are also available. This book features the Top 50 Sustainable Leaders of India.
How many copies of the Coffee Table Book are printed?
We print 5,000 premium physical copies distributed to industry leaders, government offices, and delegates. Additionally, it has a digital reach of over 5,00,000 sustainable enthusiasts.
Is there GST on Coffee Table Book pricing?
No, GST is not applicable on the current pricing for Golden preneur 2026.
